How to Use

Real-Time XBH Probability Lookup

1Note the Exit Velocity (EV) from the broadcast — find the row.
2Note the Launch Angle (LA) — find the column.
3The intersection shows XBH% — the probability that batted ball is an extra-base hit (2B, 3B, or HR).
4Use the spray angle tabs to match the batted ball direction. Spray angles are measured from center field: negative = pull side (left for RHB), positive = opposite field (right for RHB).

Spray Angle Guide

-45° to -30° — Deep pull side (down the line)
-30° to -15° — Pull side (pull gap)
-15° to 0° — Center-pull (left-center)
0° to 15° — Center-oppo (right-center)
15° to 30° — Oppo side (oppo gap)
30° to 45° — Deep oppo (down the line)

Color Key

Dark/Blue = Very unlikely XBH (0-5%)
Green = Low chance (5-15%)
Yellow/Gold = Moderate (15-30%)
Orange = Good chance (30-50%)
Red/Pink = High/certain XBH (50%+)

Data Source

MLB Statcast Balls In Play, 2023–2025. XBH = 2B + 3B + HR rate on BIP.
